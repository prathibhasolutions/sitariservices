Sitari Solutions ("we", "our", "us") provides web and mobile-accessible services for employee operations, token handling, worksheet management, communication, and related business workflows. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to users.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account and profile data, such as employee name, mobile number, department, and login details.
  • Operational data, such as worksheet entries, token records, attendance sessions, and task information.
  • Communication data, such as chat messages, assignment messages, and uploaded attachments/files.
  • Device and usage data needed for security and operations, such as IP address and session activity.
  • Location-related data where required for attendance/geofence validation features.

2. How We Use Information

  • To authenticate users and provide access to authorized features.
  • To run core services such as worksheet entry, token workflows, attendance, and reporting.
  • To improve reliability, prevent misuse, and maintain system security.
  • To communicate service updates, operational notifications, and task-related information.
  • To comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only in the following cases:

  • With authorized internal administrators and staff for business operations.
  • With technical service providers that host or support the platform under confidentiality obligations.
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect rights and security.

4. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as necessary to provide services, operate business workflows, maintain required records, and meet legal or compliance obligations. Retention periods may vary by data type.

5. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. No system can guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on applicable law, users may request access, correction, or deletion of certain personal information. For operational accounts, requests may be routed through authorized administrators.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
